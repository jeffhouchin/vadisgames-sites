Privacy Policy

App: Sudoku Challenge
Publisher: Vadis Games (Florida, USA)
Contact: support@vadisgames.com
Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Sudoku Challenge collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • We use Google sign-in (Firebase Authentication) to identify players for leaderboards, tournaments, and syncing.
  • We store gameplay data such as scores, streaks, and tournament results (Firebase Database services).
  • We use analytics and crash reporting to improve stability (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics).
  • We show a minimal banner ad (Google AdMob). Advertising identifiers may be used to serve and measure ads.
  • We may send push notifications about streaks, tournaments, and updates (Firebase Cloud Messaging).

Information we collect

1) Account information (Google Sign-In)

  • Your Google account identifier provided to the app through Firebase Authentication.
  • Your Google profile display name (and potentially a profile photo) as provided by Google, which may be shown on leaderboards/tournaments.
  • Your email address may be accessible to us through the sign-in process depending on your Google account settings and the permissions granted.

2) Gameplay & social features

  • Puzzle progress, completion status, difficulty, time, scores, streaks, and tournament participation/results.
  • Device-to-device syncing data tied to your account (e.g., saved games, results).

3) App diagnostics

  • Crash logs and performance data (e.g., crash traces, app state at crash time) via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • App usage events (e.g., screens viewed, feature usage) via Firebase Analytics.

4) Advertising data (AdMob)

  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and device information may be collected by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads.
  • We currently show a banner ad at the bottom of the screen in portrait mode.

5) Push notifications

  • A device push token (e.g., an FCM registration token) used to deliver notifications you are eligible to receive.
  • Notification interactions (e.g., opened/dismissed) may be recorded to improve relevance and reliability.

How we use information

  • To provide login, leaderboards, tournaments, and syncing across devices.
  • To operate and improve game features, including streaks and tournament scoring.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability and performance.
  • To show and measure ads (AdMob) and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • To send push notifications (e.g., streak reminders, tournament results), if enabled on your device.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information in these cases:

  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase and Google AdMob to operate the app (authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting, notifications, and ads).
  • Public display: Your Google profile display name (and possibly profile photo if provided) may appear on leaderboards/tournament results to other users.
  • Legal/safety: If required by law, or to protect users, the public, or our rights.

Data retention

  • Gameplay and tournament data may be retained as long as needed to operate leaderboards, historical results, and syncing.
  • Crash/analytics data is retained according to Firebase settings and is used to improve the app.
  • You can request deletion of account-linked data by emailing support@vadisgames.com.

Your choices

  • Sign-in: You can use Google account settings to manage what information is shared during sign-in.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in Android settings at any time.
  • Ads: You can reset or limit ad personalization from Android’s privacy/ad settings (availability varies by Android version/region).
  • Data access/deletion: Contact us to request access to or deletion of account-linked data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures and trusted providers (Firebase) to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s privacy

Sudoku Challenge is not directed to children under 13 and is intended primarily for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Third-party services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information under their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore/Realtime Database, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging)
  • Google AdMob

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the effective date above.
