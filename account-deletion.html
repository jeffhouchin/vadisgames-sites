Account Deletion Request

If you would like your Sudoku Challenge account and associated data deleted, you can request deletion using one of the methods below.

Option 1 (Recommended)

Use the account deletion option inside the app settings. This will automatically request removal of your account data.

Option 2

Email us at support@vadisgames.com with the subject line: Account Deletion Request. Please include the Google account email used to sign in.

What will be deleted

  • Leaderboard and tournament data
  • Saved puzzle progress and streaks
  • Associated account identifiers

Some anonymized analytics or crash data may remain for operational and legal purposes.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@vadisgames.com.
